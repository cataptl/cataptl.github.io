thoiry is my first artist alias. It represents my music ; an object of technology in an animal and organic world. This is the pseudonym which I use as a composer. Working with multiple acoustic instruments as well as electronic instruments. I work on the whole process of making music, from composition to production/performance and post-production so I can leave no gaps in a seamless product.

I am a producer as André Thoiry. Based in Paris. Speaking French, English and Slovenian. I work in an analogue setting as well as in a full DAW setting and I wish to give all the tools to the artists I work with to feed their ideas. Currently focused on developing my portfolio and industry experience through collaborative projects and independent releases.
